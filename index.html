Who we are

We are a couple from Mid Wales. We love dogs and love training them. We are made up of Dylan who is Head of Training and Sarah who is Head of Cuddles.

What we do

We train the dogs to a high standard tayloring each lesson to the dog. We love and care for the dog like they are our own.

Where are we based?

We are based in Llandovery. We have a variety of terrain on a lovely farm. Not only do we have sheep but we have some beautiful calves.
